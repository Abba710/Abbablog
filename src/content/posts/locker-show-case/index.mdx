# Locker: Secure Bookmark Manager for Your Browser

If your browser bookmarks have turned into a long, unorganized list of links, you're not alone. Most browsers offer only basic bookmark management, making it difficult to organize projects, separate personal and work resources, or quickly find saved pages.

Locker is a **secure bookmark manager and browser sidebar** designed to make bookmarking fast, organized, and private. Instead of relying on traditional bookmark folders, Locker provides a modern workspace where you can save links, organize them into folders, search instantly, manage browsing sessions, generate QR codes, export PDFs, and even summarize articles with AI.

Unlike many bookmark managers, Locker stores your data using **local encryption**, giving you complete control over your bookmarks while keeping them private. Whether you're a developer, student, researcher, designer, or simply someone who saves dozens of webpages every week, Locker helps you stay organized without sacrificing privacy.

This guide walks through every feature available in Locker.

# Frequently Asked Questions

## Is Locker free?

Yes. Locker includes a free version with core bookmark management features, while advanced functionality is available in the premium version.

---

## Are my bookmarks encrypted?

Yes. Locker encrypts your bookmark database locally, helping keep your data private.

---

## Does Locker support Incognito mode?

Yes. Simply enable **Allow in Incognito** in your browser's extension settings.

---

## Can I import my existing bookmarks?

Yes. Locker can import bookmarks directly from your browser, so you don't have to recreate your folders manually.

---

## Can I export my bookmarks?

Yes. You can export bookmarks for backup, migration, or sharing.

---

## Who is Locker for?

Locker is designed for developers, students, researchers, designers, marketers, and anyone who regularly saves webpages and wants a faster, more organized, and private bookmarking experience.
